Open Data

Datasets

Sequencing data from the Prins Lab deposited in NCBI GEO and other public repositories.

GSE300202

snRNAseq Analysis of PAH Liver

Single-nucleus RNA sequencing of liver tissue from PAH patients and healthy controls to evaluate the effect of PAH on the cellular landscape of the liver.

Human snRNA-seq 10 samples

Blake MJ et al. Journal of Clinical Investigation, 2026

GSE299927

Molecular Evolution of RV Dysfunction

Single-nucleus RNA sequencing of right ventricular free wall nuclei from control pigs and pigs with varying degrees of RV dysfunction.

Porcine snRNA-seq 12 samples
GSE291976

Right Atrial snRNAseq Analysis

Single-nucleus RNA sequencing characterizing the cellular landscape of the right atrium in control and pulmonary artery banded pigs.

Porcine snRNA-seq 8 samples

Mendelson JB et al. Circulation Research, 2025

GSE288047

GP130 Antagonism in Porcine RV Failure

snRNA-seq of RV free wall from control, pressure-overloaded, and SC144-treated pigs evaluating the effects of GP130 antagonism on the cellular landscape.

Porcine snRNA-seq 12 samples

Mendelson JB et al. Circulation Research, 2025

GSE277179

Ferroptosis Inhibition — Lung Transcriptomics

RNA-seq evaluating the effects of ferroptosis inhibition on lung transcriptomics in monocrotaline rats.

Rat RNA-seq 12 samples

Kazmirczak F et al. Circulation Research, 2024

GSE277180

Ferroptotic Endothelial Cell DAMPs

RNA-seq examining the effects of ferroptotic endothelial cell DAMPs on pulmonary artery smooth muscle cells.

Human RNA-seq 8 samples

Kazmirczak F et al. Circulation Research, 2024

GSE224428

Multi-omic Analysis of RV Failure

Transcriptomics and proteomics in pulmonary artery banded piglets, comparing molecular pathways of RV failure across species.

Porcine RNA-seq 8 samples

Mendelson JB et al. Journal of Heart and Lung Transplantation, 2024
