Prins Lab

Investigating the mechanisms of cardiovascular disease through integrative approaches spanning molecular biology, genomics, and translational medicine.

The Prins Lab, led by Kurt Prins MD PhD, is dedicated to advancing our understanding of disease mechanisms. We combine cutting-edge experimental and computational approaches to address fundamental questions in medicine.
