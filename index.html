Prins Lab

Investigating the mechanisms of cardiovascular disease through integrative approaches spanning molecular biology, genomics, and translational medicine.

The Prins Lab, led by Kurt Prins MD PhD, is dedicated to understanding how pulmonary arterial hypertension drives right ventricular failure and multi-organ dysfunction. We combine molecular biology, metabolomics, and large-animal models to identify new therapeutic targets.
